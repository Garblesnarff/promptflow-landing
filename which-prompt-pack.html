Common Questions About Overlap

Is there overlap between the Marketers pack and the Social Media pack?
Some, but they're different tools. The Marketers pack covers the full marketing stack: paid ads, email campaigns, SEO, analytics, competitive research, and content strategy. The Social Media pack focuses purely on platform-specific organic content: Instagram captions, TikTok scripts, LinkedIn posts, Pinterest descriptions. If you run paid social and also manage organic content, both are useful. If you only do one, pick the one that matches your work.
What's the difference between the Business pack and the Marketers pack?
Business Prompts 500 covers everything a business owner deals with — including marketing, but also hiring, finance, legal, operations, product development, and leadership. If you're a business owner wearing every hat, that's your pack. The Marketers pack goes deeper on marketing specifically — more nuance on email sequences, ad creative, SEO, and analytics. If marketing is your whole job (not one of many hats), the Marketers pack is more useful per prompt.
I'm a freelance copywriter — which pack should I get?
Probably two: the AI Copywriting Masterpack ($22) for framework-based copy prompts (AIDA, PAS, BAB), and the Writers pack ($17) for content/blog writing and editing. If you also write social media copy for clients, add the Social Media pack. Total: $56 for three specialized packs, or consider the bundle at $67 for the full toolkit including the business and Notion tools.
Should I just buy the bundle?
If you're going to buy 2+ individual packs, the bundle is the better deal — you get 7 products for $67 vs. buying them separately at $159. If you only need one specific pack, buy that one. The bundle makes sense when you have multiple use cases: you run a business, do your own marketing, manage social media, and want to be more systematic about all of it.
Can I try before I buy?
Yes — each pack has a free resource page with 20-30 real prompts from the pack. Use them to verify the quality before purchasing.
