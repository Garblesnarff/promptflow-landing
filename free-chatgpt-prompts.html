Common Questions

Are these prompts really free?
Yes — every prompt on the pages above is free to use. No email required, no signup, no catch. We make money when people find the free prompts useful and want to go deeper with a paid pack.
Which AI tool should I use with these prompts?
All prompts work with ChatGPT (free tier is fine), Claude, and Google Gemini. We recommend ChatGPT for most business tasks, Claude for longer writing and analysis, and Gemini if you're in the Google ecosystem. The prompts are written to be model-agnostic.
What's the difference between the free prompts and the paid packs?
The free pages have 20-30 prompts covering the most common use cases. The paid packs go 5-10× deeper — the Business Prompts 500, for example, has 500 prompts across 14 categories including legal, finance, hiring, and competitive strategy. The Writers Pack has 44 prompts organized specifically by the blocking situation you're in. Paid packs also include context for why each prompt works, which helps you adapt them rather than just copy-paste.
How do I use these prompts effectively?
Replace everything in [BRACKETS] with your specifics. The more specific you are, the better the output — "I run a 12-person marketing agency in Chicago specializing in B2B SaaS clients" will get a vastly better result than "I run a business." Think of the brackets as required fields, not optional suggestions.
Do I need the paid version of ChatGPT?
No. The free tier of ChatGPT (GPT-4o mini) handles all of these prompts well. ChatGPT Plus gives you access to GPT-4o and is noticeably better for complex analysis and long documents, but it's not required to get value from any prompt on this site.
