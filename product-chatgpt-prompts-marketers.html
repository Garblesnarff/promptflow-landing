5 Sample Prompts

Real prompts from the pack. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your specifics.

Content Strategy
I sell [product/service] to [target audience]. Build a 30-day content calendar: one post per day, each with a topic, platform recommendation, content angle, and CTA. Use this content mix: 60% educational, 20% social proof, 20% direct offer. Every topic must connect back to the problem [my product/service] solves.
Email Marketing
Write a 5-email welcome sequence for new subscribers who downloaded [lead magnet]. Email 1: deliver the lead magnet + one quick win. Email 2: teach the biggest mistake [your audience] makes. Email 3: a real customer result. Email 4: soft intro to [product]. Email 5: direct offer with a deadline. Each: subject line, preview text, 150-word body max. Tone: [casual/professional].
Ad Copy
Write 3 Facebook ad variants for [product/service] at [price point], targeting [describe audience]. Version 1: PAS (problem-agitate-solve). Version 2: before/after story. Version 3: bold question hook. Each ad: headline (under 40 chars), primary text (under 125 words), one-line CTA. Tone: [direct/friendly/authoritative].
SEO & Search
I create content about [topic] for [target audience]. Give me 10 long-tail keyword targets. For each: the keyword phrase, search intent (informational/commercial/transactional), estimated competition (low/medium/high), and a one-sentence article angle. Focus on keywords where the searcher is 1-2 steps from buying [product/service].
Analytics & Reporting
Here's last week's marketing data: [paste your metrics — traffic, conversions, CAC, channel breakdown]. Analyze it: What's working? What's not worth the time? What's the most surprising insight? Give me exactly 3 specific actions to take next week based on this data. Skip general advice — tell me what to actually do.
