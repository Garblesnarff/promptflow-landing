5 Sample Prompts

Real prompts from the pack. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your product details.

Product Descriptions — Shopify
Write a Shopify product description for [product name]. Lead with a one-sentence hook that focuses on the transformation or outcome — not the product. Then: 2-sentence problem statement, 3-sentence solution (what makes this product different), bullet list of 5 key features written as customer benefits. End with a one-line CTA. Tone: [casual/professional/aspirational]. Product details: [describe your product].
Abandoned Cart Email
Write an abandoned cart recovery email for [store name / product left in cart]. Subject line: curiosity-based, not pushy — make them wonder what they almost missed. Body: acknowledge they're busy (not accusatory), remind them what's in the cart in one vivid sentence, add one trust signal (return policy / review / guarantee), end with a single clear CTA button. Total: 120 words max. Tone: warm, not desperate.
Facebook Ad — Product Launch
Write 3 Facebook ad variations for [product name] launching [date / "this week"]. Variation 1: lead with the problem. Variation 2: lead with a specific before/after outcome. Variation 3: lead with social proof (you'll add a real quote, just write "[[customer quote here]]" as a placeholder). All three should have the same CTA: [your CTA]. Product: [describe it]. Target audience: [describe buyer]. Budget assumption: testing at $10/day.
Customer Review Response
Write a response to this [3-star / 2-star / 1-star] review: "[paste review]". Rules: (1) open by thanking them by first name, (2) acknowledge the specific issue they mentioned — don't be defensive, (3) explain what we're doing about it or offer a direct solution, (4) invite them to reach out privately to [email]. Tone: professional, empathetic, not robotic. Under 80 words.
Amazon Listing SEO Title
Write 5 Amazon product title options for [product]. Each title must: (1) start with the brand name "[Brand]", (2) include the primary keyword "[main keyword]", (3) include 2-3 secondary keywords: [list them], (4) mention a key benefit or differentiator, (5) stay under 200 characters. Rank the 5 options by click-through potential.
