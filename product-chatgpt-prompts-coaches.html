5 Sample Prompts

Real prompts from the pack. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your specifics.

Discovery Call
Write a 30-minute discovery call script for a [COACHING NICHE] coach working with [IDEAL CLIENT]. Structure: (1) Warm opening and agenda (2 min), (2) Understanding their current situation (8 min), (3) Uncovering the real pain point — not just the surface problem (8 min), (4) Painting the vision of where they want to go (5 min), (5) Explaining your process and next step (5 min), (6) Handling "I need to think about it" (2 min). Include open-ended questions for each section. Do not make it salesy — the goal is genuine discovery.
Proposal Writing
Write a professional coaching proposal for a [PROGRAM LENGTH] [COACHING NICHE] program priced at [$PRICE]. Include: client's name and the specific challenge they described, the transformation you're promising (outcome), how the program works (structure, sessions, support), what's included, investment and payment options, the next step and deadline, and a brief bio. Tone: warm but authoritative. Not a brochure — a specific response to their situation.
Difficult Conversation
Write a response email for a client who is angry and sends a heated message expressing frustration with the coaching process or their lack of results. The email should: acknowledge their frustration without defensiveness, thank them for sharing, ask a genuine clarifying question, and propose a conversation to work through it. Do not explain yourself, justify, or get pulled into the content of their complaint in writing. Under 200 words. Tone: grounded, professional, caring.
Marketing — Email Sequence
Write a 5-email welcome sequence for new subscribers who downloaded [LEAD MAGNET NAME]. Email 1: deliver the lead magnet + warm intro. Email 2: share a belief or philosophy that sets you apart. Email 3: a story (yours or a client's) that demonstrates the problem you solve. Email 4: value-add resource or tip. Email 5: soft pitch for your core offer with a deadline or limited availability. Write full subject lines, preview text, and body copy for each.
Session Tool — Vision Casting
Write a "vision casting" session script (20 minutes) to use when a client loses motivation. Guide them through: imagining their life/business in 12 months if everything works out, what they see, feel, and have, who's around them, what they've stopped doing, and what they've started. Include sensory language prompts. End with: "What's one thing this vision tells you about what matters most?"
