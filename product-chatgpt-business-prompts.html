5 Sample Prompts

These are real prompts from the pack. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your situation.

Business Strategy
Analyze my business: [describe what you do, who you serve, current revenue]. What are the 3 biggest opportunities I'm not pursuing, and the 3 biggest risks I'm ignoring? Be direct — I need an honest outside perspective, not encouragement.
Sales & Revenue
Write 3 responses to this objection: "[paste the objection]". I sell [product/service] at [price]. Response 1: reframe the concern. Response 2: use social proof. Response 3: ask a clarifying question to find the real objection underneath.
Hiring & Team
I'm interviewing for [role]. Based on this candidate summary: [paste resume or notes], write 5 behavioral interview questions that test for [specific skill]. For each question, describe what a strong answer sounds like and what a weak answer sounds like.
Financial Planning
I'm considering raising prices from [current] to [new] for [product/service]. I have [X] customers with [Y%] average retention. Model the financial impact: at what churn rate does the price increase break even? Show me the math at 5%, 10%, 15%, and 20% customer loss.
AI & Productivity
My task list today: [paste your list]. My most important goal this week: [goal]. Reorder this list by impact × urgency. Flag anything I should delegate or cut entirely. Give me a realistic time estimate for what remains.
