3 Sample Prompts

Real prompts from the pack. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your specifics.

Instagram · Captions
Write an Instagram caption for a [NICHE] account about [TOPIC]. Open with a single-line hook that stops scrolling — one sentence that creates curiosity or speaks directly to [AUDIENCE'S PAIN POINT]. Deliver real value in 3-4 short sentences. End with an engagement question that's easy to answer. Include 15 relevant hashtags. Tone: [YOUR BRAND VOICE]. Keep paragraphs to 1-2 lines max.
TikTok · Scripts
Write a 60-second TikTok script for [TOPIC] targeting [AUDIENCE]. Structure: 0-3s hook (stop the scroll), 3-10s promise (what they'll learn), 10-50s value delivery (3 specific tips, 10-15 seconds each), 50-60s CTA (comment, follow, or link). Requirements: conversational tone that sounds like talking not reading, each tip specific and actionable. Add notes about gestures or visuals for each section.
Content Strategy · Repurposing
I posted this on [PLATFORM]: [PASTE YOUR POST]. Repurpose it into 5 formats: (1) Instagram caption with hook + hashtags, (2) LinkedIn post with professional angle, (3) Twitter thread 5-7 tweets, (4) TikTok script 30 seconds, (5) email newsletter intro under 200 words. Keep the core insight but adapt format, length, and tone for each platform's culture and audience expectations.
