Setup in 5 Steps (~30 Minutes)

  1. Duplicate the template (5 min) — click the link in your download, hit Duplicate in Notion, choose your workspace

  3. Add active projects (5 min) — open Project Tracker, add every project with status, deadline, client, and one next action
  4. Log outstanding invoices (5 min) — open Invoice Tracker, add any unpaid invoices with amounts and due dates
  5. Customize your workflow stages (5 min) — rename the default stages to match how you actually work
