5 Sample Prompts

Real prompts from the pack. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your specifics.

Property Listing Description
Write a luxury property listing for [address / property type]. Open with an experience — what does it feel like to walk in the front door? Do NOT lead with square footage or bedroom count. Second paragraph: 3 most compelling features, each described with one sensory detail. Third paragraph: location lifestyle (what does this neighborhood give the buyer?). Close: 1 sentence that makes the buyer feel the opportunity. Total: 200 words. Tone: aspirational but not overwrought.
Seller Follow-up After Low Offer
Write an email to my seller client after we received an offer at [offer price] vs. our list price of [list price]. Goals: (1) validate their frustration without dismissing it, (2) explain the real market context in 2 sentences, (3) present our options (counter, reject, accept) as a decision they own, (4) recommend what I think we should do and briefly why. Tone: honest, direct, confident. Not a lecture. Under 200 words.
Instagram Market Update Post
Write an Instagram caption for a local real estate market update. Data to use: [paste your current stats — median price, days on market, etc.]. Format: Start with a bold hook (1 line). Then 3-4 lines of insight framed as "what this means for buyers/sellers." End with a question that prompts comments. Include line breaks after each idea. Under 150 words. Relevant hashtags: 5-8, include [your city], [neighborhood], and real estate terms. Professional but conversational — like a smart friend, not a press release.
Multiple Offer Script
Write a script for a call with my buyer client where I explain we're in a multiple offer situation on [property]. I need to: (1) communicate the competitive situation without panic, (2) explain what makes an offer competitive right now — specifically [waiving inspection / escalation clause / cash / quick close], (3) ask them to make a clear decision (how aggressive do they want to be?). Make it a conversation, not a monologue. Include natural pause points marked "[pause]" where I should listen.
CMА / Market Analysis Summary
Write a 2-paragraph market analysis summary for a seller presentation using this data: [paste your comps data]. Paragraph 1: what the data shows about current market conditions (be specific with numbers). Paragraph 2: what this means for pricing strategy — give a price range recommendation with reasoning, not just a number. Tone: authoritative but accessible. Avoid jargon. This will be presented to a seller who doesn't read real estate reports daily.
