The 8 Modules, Explained

📥 Inbox
The entry point for everything. Paste a URL, dictate a thought, drop a file reference — the inbox captures it without requiring any organization decision in the moment. Weekly review moves items to their permanent home.
📋 Projects Dashboard
Everything with a deadline lives here. Kanban view for status, timeline view for planning, and a "next action" field on every project so you always know exactly what to do next — not just that the project exists.
🎯 Areas of Responsibility
Long-term commitments with no end date: health, finances, relationships, career development. Each area links to relevant projects and resources so context is never more than two clicks away.
📚 Resource Library
The knowledge layer. Bookmarks, articles, research, reference material — organized by topic with a notes field for your synthesis. Tagging and filtering built in.
🤖 AI Prompt Library (new in 2026)
Most AI users lose their best prompts to chat history. This module gives you a searchable database: prompt title, purpose, the prompt text, and a "last used" field. Your best prompts get better over time because you can find and refine them.
