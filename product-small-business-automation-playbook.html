3 Sample Prompts from the Pack

Real prompts from the playbook. Replace anything in [BRACKETS] with your specifics.

Ch. 2 — Customer Service FAQ Response
You are a customer service agent for [BUSINESS NAME], a [TYPE OF BUSINESS]. A customer has sent the following message: [PASTE CUSTOMER EMAIL]. Write a professional, friendly response that: (1) acknowledges their concern specifically, (2) answers their question directly using this information: [PASTE YOUR POLICY/ANSWER], (3) offers one next step. Keep it under 150 words. Sign off as [YOUR NAME].
Ch. 3 — Weekly Content Batch
Create 5 social media posts for [BUSINESS NAME], a [BUSINESS TYPE] that helps [TARGET CUSTOMER] [ACHIEVE OUTCOME]. This week's theme: [TOPIC/PROMOTION]. For each post: write a platform version for (1) Instagram (casual, 150 words max, include a hook as the first line), (2) LinkedIn (professional insight angle, 200 words max). Avoid promotional language in posts 1–3. Post 4 can mention a product or service. Post 5 should ask a question to drive engagement.
Ch. 6 — Proposal Generator
Write a professional service proposal for a potential client. Business: [YOUR BUSINESS NAME]. Service offered: [DESCRIBE SERVICE]. Client's stated goal: [WHAT THEY TOLD YOU THEY WANT]. Client's context: [INDUSTRY, SIZE, CHALLENGE]. Proposal should include: (1) executive summary (3 sentences), (2) understanding of their problem, (3) proposed solution and deliverables, (4) timeline (3 phases with milestones), (5) investment (use [PRICE] for total, payment terms: [YOUR TERMS]), (6) next steps. Professional but not stiff. First-person from me to them.
